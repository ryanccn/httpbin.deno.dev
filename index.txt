Welcome to httpbin.deno.dev!
============================

This is a basic port of `httpbin` to Deno.

Here are some endpoints that you can test out:

- /get
- /post
- /patch
- /put
- /delete

- /auth/basic/:username/:password
- /auth/bearer

- /status/:code

- /ip
- /user-agent
- /headers

- /redirect?to=SOME_URL

Have fun!
